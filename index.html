Our Purpose

As students who have gone through the college process we know the stress and confusion that comes along with it, which is why we have created multiple resources to help you choose the best path for you! Our goal is to create a safe and positive environment that takes the pressure off of the future, showing you multiple routes and options you can take after high school graduation. Embedded in this website is everything you need to accomplish and develop your future goals, like fun interactive explorers, scholarship and fafsa links, student advice sections, and so much more!


Our Goal In Depth


This site is designed to be a tool for younger students who are considering their futures. It is very stressful especially in the ever changing climate of education and career decisions, so this is more of a way to present multitudes of information in interesting, clear ways for young people to find what could potentially be the best next life-decision for them. Overall we want to create a reliable, engaging, and most importantly a low stress climate that will help future generations of students pinpoint what their life will be like after high school. By providing students with guidance and support we hope to ignite a spark, an idea, a plan, within them; making this process, whether college bound or not, less vulnerable and scary.
