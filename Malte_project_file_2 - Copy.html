Why Should You Start Looking for Colleges?

Don’t be scared! All the tools on this page are here to help you and make your college application and search process so much easier! Although starting your college search process may be scary at first, remember you're starting one of the most exciting times of your life! Although there are thousands of colleges out there, this page will help you narrow it down and help you to characterize your search to what fits best for you! Continue scrolling to take our “choose your own adventure” style quiz, and get a list of values that cater specifically to your responses!

How To Use The Quiz:

Go through and take your time answering the questions below. Each question corresponds to a certain value that is important when starting to look for colleges! (Some values include: diversity, academic rigor, location, etc) After finishing the quiz, you will get a list of values that are the most important to you based on how you answered the questions. Using these values, you can then use them to cater your college search specifically to you!

Why is this helpful?

Our list of values is: Academic Opportunities, Social Life, Living Arrangements, Location, School Size, Academic Rigor, and Financials . These different values are important for you to know because some colleges are better than others at specific things. Although none of these questions will narrow down your college search to one perfect college for you, it might help you to consider contributing factors that are not always taken into account. Put these values into other sites’ college search engines or into our own, they might help to find the perfect college for you!
